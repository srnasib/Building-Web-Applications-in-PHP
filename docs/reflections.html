Reflections

The Clever Swabian

02 July 2019

Welcome to the blog of team The Clever Swabian! We are excited to show you the final stages of our project. We developed our first Android application. On this page, we are going to inform you about technical challenges that all of us encountered during work , benefits the team experienced and how the ISEE course met our needs.

Technical challenges

  • Lack of experience regarding Android developlemt
    The team members of The clever Swabian hardly had any beforehand experience with Android or application development in general. Two of us are from the mechanical engineering, one from electrical engineering and one from CSE (he doesn't have that much experience in Android) which led to some problems. Most of us tried to fill up this knowledge gap and all of us gathered a lot of knowledge about Java and Android Studio. In the very end we can say that we all as a team have always tried to give the best of it.

  • Unavailability of proper documentation
    There are numerous tutorials/documentations for Android Studio available, however these are often outdated due to the rapid further development of Android. After getting any documentation we had to go through heavy research to make it compatible to our app and that happened in almost every case. This didn’t help to make development easier.

  • Android Studio takes a lot of space (1.64 GB)
    After starting Android Studio and launching Android Emulator, more than 90% of physical memory has been used by only these two. That’s why working with Android Studio often took us more time than expected.

    • Team Challenges

    Apart from technical difficulties we faced some challenges caused by the team itself.

    • Bringing the team together
      A team achieves more when all of the members are fully engaged and interested in the outcome. Sometimes, it is hard to bring all the members under the same roof. At first, designating weekly meeting slots was a bit challenging, as we did not have a common timetable. Distinguishing the range of abilities of each colleague and appointing errands as needed was also challenging.

    • Task management
      As few of our colleagues had some thought regarding programming advancement even before the begin, the undertaking of the rest was not so challenging. Isolating undertakings in every milestone and appointing to colleagues made work adjusted. Everybody left a mark in each progression of this android application. Lastly, in light of the profitable direction from our customer/instructor, we could finish every one of our assignments on time.

    Benefits

    When it comes to maximizing the effectiveness of a project, no matter how big or small, teamwork can improve just about every aspect of its performance.

    • Efficiency
      When a team works well together as a unit, they are able to accomplish more than its individual members can do alone. Every member applied different skills, so we were able to come up with a more effective solution.

    • Better outcomes
      Mutual support can have the benefit of encouraging people to achieve goals they may not have realized they could reach on their own. Information is shared among the team members, maximizing the levels of knowledge and learning. The stronger team members effectively improve the weaker.

    • New ideas
      Everyone can gain knowledge and ideas from each other. After completing this project, we can ensure that now we know tons of new things/techniques that were unknown to us before this project. Whenever some team members were stuck in some point, others tried their best to pull him up by sharing their ideas. So by this process, all of us has gathered tremendous knowledge about many things. We hope, this will help a lot in our future work.

    About the course

    When it comes to maximizing the effectiveness of a project, no matter how big or small, the course was as expected a great platform to have hands on experience in developing an android app and learning some project management skills. It was a more effective learning approach, due to practical application of the lecture contents. It has helped us in gaining additional programming skills.

    • Software engineering
      Learning how an entire software engineering project, from requirements analysis to the testing of the product, can look in reality.

    • Agile method applying
      Learning and applying agile software project management with SCRUM.

    • Frist hand customer experience
      Knowing how the interactions with the customer can take place, and how we need to understand and adapt to customer’s exact needs.

    We would like to thank Dr. Schulze for affording us with the opportunity to learn in such a practical way. He brought out the best in us by his regular feedbacks, continuous support and commitment to sharing his knowledge
