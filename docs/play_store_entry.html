The Clever Swabian

03 July 2019

Welcome to the blog of team The Clever Swabian! We are a team of four students who are excited to show you the final status of our developed application. On this page, we are introducing you about the Play Store entry of our app.

How our app looks in the Playstore:

Main Features:

Add a new income or expense entry into the app :

Anytime you can add an expense or income into the app very simply. There are quite a lot income and expense categories which covers most common aspects of our life. Also with a lot of Payment categories you can define payment type in each entry. The Home screen is there to display your monthy balance, warnings based on the expense threshold you have set and your last three entries.

List of Entries, Sorting & Filtering of Entries :

From the list of Entries menu you can also Sort and Filter your saved Entries or Transactions. For Sorting there are quiet a lot of methods to sort the entries. For Filtering a starting date and ending date can be entered, you can also give your preferred category and payment type at the same time to Filter the entries.

Add new Recurring Incomes and Expenses :

You have an expense or income that is repeated in every month? Then our recurring entry option is here to make your app experience more smoother. From this menu you can add your recurring income or expense, set the starting or ending date for it and ofcourse you can set the interval period for this recurring entry.

Add new thresholds to get warning for a specific category :

Sometimes we are unaware or unconsious about an expense of a specific category and we exaust the limit we had thought in our mind. To get control over this problem we have introduced the option to set multiple warnings where you can set the upper and lower threshold value for getting warnings for a specific expense category. The home screen will update automatically and display you a warning message when the upper threshold is nearly or fully exausted. When the expense is too low for the specific category based on the lower threshold it will also display a warning message at the home screen.

For your data security you can set your own username and password :

For privacy concerns we have integrated the Security settings so while starting the app for the first time the user will be asked to set up his Username and Password. If you skip at the first time you can later set this from the security menu. After the usernsame & password has been successfully set up every time the user exits the app he will be asked to enter his username & password to enter the app. Security and privacy of the app has always been a major concern for us.

Now you can Create your own Monthly Report :

You can create your monthly report from the app and view or save it as a pdf file. The pdf file will contain all your monthly expense and income breakdown. You can also share this pdf file using different services like mail, Whatsapp, Messenger etc.
