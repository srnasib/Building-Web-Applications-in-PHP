About our app

Our app is called just like our team "The clever Swabian".
It's a daily use app that's useful for tracking down all your expenses and incomes and thus giving you a perfect situation overview.

Spending money is easy, but taking control over it is oftentimes a challenge. This app is designed and implemented to help with the latter. It allows users to manually and/or automatically specify what they spent their money for. Given some income that can be specified as well, it enables you at any time to double-check whether your budget is in balance or not. Moreover, maintaining a history of your inputs allows to observe how your behaviour has changed, which is especially useful to get rid of imbalances.
